The Core Logic

The Goal

Are two variables related? Under Independence, the distribution of one variable does not change based on the category of the other.

\( H_0 \): Independence

"No difference in distribution; variables are unrelated."

\( H_a \): Dependence

"Distributions differ; a significant association exists."
