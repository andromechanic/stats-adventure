The Hook

Linear Regression is the mathematical process of drawing the "Line of Best Fit" through a cloud of data points. It assumes a linear relationship between an Independent Variable ($x$) and a Dependent Variable ($y$).

02

Ordinary Least Squares

\( \hat{y} = \beta_0 + \beta_1 x + \epsilon \)

Minimizing the Sum of Squared Residuals

The "Best Fit" is the line where the total area of the "Error Squares" (the distance between points and the line) is at its absolute minimum. This is why we call it Least Squares.

04. The Modeling Verdict

The Correlation ($r$)

Ranges from -1 to +1. A value of +1 is a perfect upward line; -1 is a perfect downward line; 0 is total chaos.

The Impact

Regression isn't just about drawing lines; it's about Quantifying Relationships. It tells exactly how much $y$ changes for every 1-unit increase in $x$.
