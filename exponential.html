01

The Hook

"How long do I have to wait between bus arrivals if they average 3 per hour?"

The Exponential distribution is the continuous analogue of the Geometric distribution. It models the time or distance between occurrences of random events in a Poisson process.

\( f(x; \lambda) = \lambda e^{-\lambda x} \)
