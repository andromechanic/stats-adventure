The Hook

"How many emails will arrive between 10 AM and 11 AM if the average is 5 per hour?"

The Poisson distribution is the discrete probability of a number of events occurring in a fixed interval of time or space, provided these events occur with a known constant mean rate and independently of the time since the last event.

The Mathematical Secret

The most elegant property of Poisson is that its Expected Value (\(\mu\)) is exactly equal to its Variance (\(\sigma^2\)). Both are just \(\lambda\).

04. The Statistical Verdict

When to Use?

Use Poisson when you have a Fixed Interval and Independent Events. It's the go-to tool for supply chain management, traffic engineering, and radioactive decay studies.
