F-Distribution

Comparing Variances and Testing Groups (ANOVA Foundations)

01

The Hook

"Is one group significantly more variable than another?"

The F-distribution was named after Sir Ronald Fisher. It arises when we compare the ratio of two scaled independent Chi-Square distributions. It is the engine of ANOVA (Analysis of Variance).

\( F = \frac{\chi_1^2 / d_1}{\chi_2^2 / d_2} \)
